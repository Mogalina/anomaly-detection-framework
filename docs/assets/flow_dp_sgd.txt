        Opt->>Opt: 3a. Clip Gradients: g = g * (C / ||g||_2)
    else ||g||_2 <= Clipping Norm (C)
        Opt->>Opt: 3b. Keep Gradients: g = g
    end
    
    Opt->>Opt: 4. Generate Gaussian Noise ~ N(0, σ²C²)
    Opt->>Opt: 5. Add Noise to Clipped Gradients
    
    Opt->>Model: 6. Update Local Weights
    
    Note over Model,Coord: Round Completion
    Model-->>Coord: 7. Transmit Privacy-Preserving Weights
